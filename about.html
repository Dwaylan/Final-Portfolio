About me

I am a Texas native born and raised in Seguin,TX. Most of my life has been a northbound journey on i-35 seeing as I am an alumnus of Texas State University, which is only a 30 minute drive north of my hometown, and now I am a resident of travis county Austin which (you guessed it) is a 30 minute drive north of my Alma Mater. 27 years of life contained on a hour and a half drive. On this life long drive I have discovered my passions in Opera, Musical Theatre, sports, and now Web Development.
